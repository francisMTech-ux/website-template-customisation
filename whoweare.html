Who We Are - Francis Mwangi Foundation

Our Vision

A world where every young person thrives in body, mind, and spirit, empowered to reach their full potential and positively impact their communities.

Our Mission

To create innovative wellness programs, foster safe spaces, and provide educational resources that encourage healthy living, resilience, and emotional growth in the youth.

Our Core Values

Empathy

We strive to understand and support the unique challenges faced by young people.

Inclusivity

We believe every young person deserves the opportunity to thrive, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Resilience

We nurture mental and emotional strength to help youth overcome life’s challenges.

Growth

We are committed to personal development and helping the youth achieve their full potential.

Community

We foster collaboration and a sense of belonging to strengthen youth engagement and wellness.

What We Do

The Francis Mwangi Foundation offers:

  • Wellness Workshops: Teaching mindfulness, physical fitness, nutrition, and mental health practices.
  • Counseling Services: Providing access to mental health professionals who offer guidance and support.
  • Skill-Building Programs: Developing practical life skills, from emotional intelligence to leadership and communication.
  • Peer Mentorship: Creating networks of youth supporting each other’s growth and well-being.
