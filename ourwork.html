Our Work - Francis Mwangi Foundation

What We Do

1. Wellness Workshops

Our workshops focus on providing young people with the tools they need to take charge of their physical and mental health. Key areas include:

  • Mindfulness & Mental Health: Relaxation techniques and coping mechanisms for stress.
  • Physical Health: Fitness education, nutritional tips, and the importance of staying active.
  • Healthy Habits: Balancing life demands with sustainable wellness practices.

2. Counseling and Mental Health Support

We offer access to licensed mental health professionals through:

  • Individual Therapy: Tailored one-on-one sessions to address personal challenges.
  • Group Therapy: Peer-led sessions to share experiences and support one another.
  • 24/7 Crisis Support: A helpline offering immediate emotional assistance.

3. Skill-Building Programs

Our skill-building programs focus on:

  • Leadership Development: Nurturing communication, decision-making, and leadership qualities.
  • Financial Literacy: Educating youth on budgeting, saving, and entrepreneurship.
  • Career Mentorship: Providing guidance on career and educational paths.

4. Youth Peer Mentorship

Our peer mentorship programs foster:

  • Self-Confidence: Building self-esteem by learning from peers.
  • Collaboration Skills: Encouraging teamwork and mutual support.
  • Community Engagement: Engaging youth in community-led initiatives for positive change.

5. Outreach and Advocacy

We advocate for the mental and physical well-being of youth through:

  • Mental Health Awareness Campaigns: Destigmatizing mental health and promoting open discussions.
  • Youth Wellness Days: Organizing fitness classes, screenings, and wellness workshops.
  • Policy Advocacy: Collaborating with government and NGOs to influence youth-friendly policies.
